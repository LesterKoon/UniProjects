New Year Traditions

From mouth-watering treats to impressive pyrotechnics, these are the ways that people commonly celebrate Chinese New Year around the world. However, the actual traditions might be slightly different. Lets take a look.

Spring Cleaning & Putting Up Decorations

The belief in this tradition is that when you clean the house,you will clear away bad luck and welcome good fortune

The Reunion Dinner

Takes place on the eve of the Lunar New Year and is a good time for family members to bond, as many would have returned to their hometowns for the festivities.

Tossing Yee Sang

The higher one tosses their Yee Sang, the more good luck, good health and good fortune they will get for the entire year

Visiting Others

It is customary to pay a visit to the homes of relatives and friends over Chinese New Year, a tradition known as ‘bai nian’

Lion Dance & Dragon Dance

The dance mimics a lion's or a dragon's movement with a hope to bring good luck and fortune

Red Packets

Given as tokens of good wishes during auspicious occasions
