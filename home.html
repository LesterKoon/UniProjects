What is Chinese New Year?

New Year, also known by the name of Lunar New Year or the Spring Festival perhaps the biggest and most important annual festival for Chinese and the Chinese communities world wide. The event is celebrated on the first day of the Chinese Lunar Calendar to usher out the old year and bring forth the luck and prosperity of the new one.
